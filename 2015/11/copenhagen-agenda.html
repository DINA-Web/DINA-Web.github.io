Copenhagen Agenda

01 November 2015

Agenda

DINA Technical workshop 2-6 Nov 2015 Copenhagen

The workshop will be held in the Zoological Museum, Universitetsparken 15 in the meeting room.

The meeting will start with the lunch at 12:00 on Monday, which will be held in August Krogh Institute (Universitetsparken 13, next door to the musem). If you’re early and/or want to drop of suitcases etc, go to the Museum main entry. The museum does not have a reception, but the staff in the museum shop will be informed about the meeting and can show you the way to the meeting room. If anything goes wrong you can call Martin Stein on +45 222 559 00.
